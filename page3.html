Turkish Hazelnut exports

Turkey is responsible for 75% of the worlds Hazelnut exports. Nuts are commonly used in many Turkish dishes such as Baklava, and you can often find turkish delights with hazelnut. Another cool fact is my grandfather is a hazelnut farmer and produces a lot of hazlenuts for companies like nutella.
